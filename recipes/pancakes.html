pancakes

Description

Delicious stuff, done in 5 minutes

  • 1 egg
  • pinch of salt
  • 1 tablespoon wholegrain spelt flour
  • 1/2 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1-3 tablespoons milk

  1. heat the pan on medium to high heat
  2. whisk eg and salt in a bowl until light in color
  3. add flour, starch and powder
  4. mix and add milk until smooth and running slowly when pouring
  5. bake for 2 minutes in the pan, then flip
